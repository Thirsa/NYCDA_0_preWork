Welcome to the site with the text of one of my favorite childhood songs!

My aunt used to sing that to me when I was little
So, here goes


She sailed away
On a sunny summer day,
On the back of a crocodile.

“You see,” said she,
“He's as tame as he can be,
I'll ride him down the Nile.”

Well, the croc winked his eye
As she waved them all goodbye,
earing a happy smile.

At the end of the ride,
The lady was inside,
And the smile
Was on the crocodile!
